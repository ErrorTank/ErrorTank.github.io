The Begin Of Our Road

How it all started...

"On May 23rd, our teacher - Mr. Nguyen Duc Dan, gave all students of TI29 an assignment: To form a group and make your own website. We - the four original members, quickly formed a group: Tạ Tuấn Hoàng, Vũ Tuấn Anh, Đinh Xuân Thắng and Nguyễn Thành Đạt. We discussed, and soon after had decided that we shall make it as big and grand as we could. During the project, on June 7th, we welcome three more members to our group: Nguyễn Phú Quang Anh, Tạ Nguyên Ngọc, and Hoàng Thanh Sơn. Everyone joined, each with their own ideas. Together, we formed a vision for our project. And our project begins..."

-Tạ Tuấn Hoàng.
